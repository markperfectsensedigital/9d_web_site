Sign the petition

Use this form to download a petition with your name filled in (sample).

  1. Search for your voter registration using the fields below. Use the name and address where last registered to vote, which may be different from your current name or address.
  2. Mark the checkbox to include the name in the petition.
  3. Select one of the names to be the circulator[?].
  4. Click Submit to download and print the completed petition.
  5. Each voter signs and dates the petition, and the circulator signs and dates at the bottom.
  6. Mail the completed petition to Nine Districts for Montgomery County, PO Box 437, Glen Echo, MD 20812-0437.
